# InvictaTill Browser 2.1.1

InvictaTill Browser 2.1.1 guarantees persistent logins and workspace names across restarts, fixes Google Meet screen sharing, and introduces a Cross-Workspace Password Manager!

## Highlights

- **Persistent Workspace Logins**: Gmail, Google, GitHub, and work accounts stay signed in after closing and reopening the browser.
- **Persistent Workspace Names**: Renamed workspace titles (`Default`, `Work`, `Personal`, and custom workspaces) now remain permanently saved across restarts.
- **Google Meet Screen Share Fix**: Refined display media handler to stream full screens and windows smoothly in Google Meet, Zoom, and Teams.
- **Cross-Workspace Password Vault 🔑**: Save credentials once securely and autofill them across all your workspaces.
- **Visible Tab Titles**: Restored tab title DOM element rendering and hostname fallback formatting on all web tabs.
- **Drag-and-Drop Reordering**: Drag and drop workspace tabs or web tabs to reorder their position.
