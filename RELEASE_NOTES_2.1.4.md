# InvictaTill Browser 2.1.4

InvictaTill Browser 2.1.4 introduces an Opera-style interactive Screen Share Picker Modal, allowing you to choose InvictaTill Tabs, Application Windows, or your Entire Screen display when presenting in Google Meet, Zoom, or Teams!

## Highlights

- **Opera-Style Screen Share Picker Modal**: When clicking "Present now" in Google Meet or Teams, InvictaTill Browser displays an interactive modal overlay to select your target share source.
- **Tab, Window & Screen Selection**:
  - **InvictaTill Tabs**: Lists all open tabs across all your workspaces.
  - **Application Window**: Lists open desktop application windows.
  - **Your Entire Screen**: Lists full display monitors.
- **Live Preview & Audio Toggle**: Includes live item preview thumbnail and "Also share tab audio" checkbox.
- **Persistent Workspace Logins**: Session cookies remain saved across restarts.
- **Cross-Workspace Password Vault 🔑**: Save and autofill credentials across all workspaces.
