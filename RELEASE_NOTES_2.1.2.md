# InvictaTill Browser 2.1.2

InvictaTill Browser 2.1.2 resolves Google Meet screen sharing (`StartupCode 219 DisconnectedError`) and adds Chromium usermedia screen capturing switches!

## Highlights

- **Guaranteed Google Meet Screen Sharing**: Resolved Google Meet's `StartupCode 219 DisconnectedError` by enabling Chromium's `enable-usermedia-screen-capturing` flags and passing display streams cleanly.
- **Persistent Workspace Logins**: Gmail, Google, GitHub, and work accounts stay signed in after closing and reopening the browser.
- **Persistent Workspace Names**: Renamed workspace titles (`Default`, `Work`, `Personal`, and custom workspaces) remain permanently saved across restarts.
- **Cross-Workspace Password Vault 🔑**: Save credentials once securely and autofill them across all your workspaces.
- **Visible Tab Titles**: Restored tab title DOM element rendering and hostname fallback formatting on all web tabs.
- **Drag-and-Drop Reordering**: Drag and drop workspace tabs or web tabs to reorder their position.
